About YOL ASIA

Who We Are

YOL ASIA is a premier mining and earth-stripping company with over a decade of experience in large-scale mining operations across Asia. We specialize in comprehensive stripping, extraction, and site management services for leading mining corporations.

Our Mission

To provide world-class mining and stripping solutions that maximize operational efficiency while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To become Asia's most trusted partner in mining operations, recognized for innovation, reliability, and sustainable practices in the extractive industry.

Company History

Founded in 2010, YOL ASIA began with a single vision: to revolutionize mining operations through cutting-edge technology and dedicated expertise. Over the years, we've grown from a regional player to a continent-wide operation, managing over 50 major projects and partnering with Asia's largest mining corporations.
